How CineClash Works

CineClash is a daily movie trivia platform with six games: Movie Grid, Cast Connections, Plotle, Moviedle, Spotlight, and Impostor. Every day has a new puzzle set and one combined score card.

The games are designed for social competition: finish your run, share your result, and challenge friends with your invite link to beat your score.
